Our story

Founded on craftsmanship, built on trust.

Sahanines Interiors was founded in 2013 by a small group of designers and second-generation carpenters who believed India's interior market was underserved on two things: honest pricing and honest craftsmanship.

Twelve years later, we've grown into a full-service studio of 60+ designers, project managers, engineers and craftsmen — but the founding principle is unchanged. Every quote is itemised, every timeline is written, every square foot is warranted.

We work on 40 projects a year — never more — because personal attention is not scalable. Our senior designer is on every project from day one to handover.

Mission

Deliver exceptional interiors — on time, on budget, without compromise.

To make premium interior design accessible, transparent and trustworthy for every homeowner and business that trusts us with their space.

Vision

Be the most trusted name in premium interior craftsmanship in India.

To set the standard for how interior design is scoped, priced, delivered and warranted — and to raise the bar for every studio that follows.

Our process

Six steps from moodboard to move-in.

01

Discovery Call

30-minute consultation to understand lifestyle, brief, budget and timelines.

02

Site Visit & Survey

Measurement, technical audit, lighting analysis and existing-condition report.

03

Design & 3D

Moodboard, floor plans and photoreal 3D walkthrough. Two revision rounds.

04

Itemised Quote

Line-by-line quote with brand names, grades, quantities and warranties.

05

Execution

On-site project manager, daily WhatsApp updates and milestone-based billing.

06

Handover & Warranty

Snag-free handover with warranty card and after-sales AMC (optional).
