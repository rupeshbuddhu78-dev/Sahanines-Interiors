Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sahanines Interiors ("we", "us") collects, uses, discloses and safeguards your information when you visit our website or engage our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the personal information you voluntarily provide via our contact and booking forms — name, phone number, email address, city, service interest, budget and any reference images or project details you share. We also collect anonymised analytics (device, browser, referrer, path).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide requested quotations
  • To schedule site visits and consultations
  • To improve our services and website performance
  • To send occasional service updates (with your consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with (a) our own team, (b) service providers under confidentiality (Cloudinary for image hosting, MongoDB Atlas for data), and (c) authorities when required by law.

5. Cookies

We use minimal, first-party cookies for authentication and session management on the admin dashboard. No third-party advertising cookies are set.

6. Data Retention

Contact and booking data is retained for up to 24 months from your last interaction, after which it is deleted from our active systems.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by writing to sahanines01@gmail.com.

8. Security

We employ industry-standard security measures — HTTPS everywhere, JWT-based authentication, bcrypt password hashing, rate limiting, XSS protection and input sanitisation.

9. Contact

Questions? Reach us at sahanines01@gmail.com.
